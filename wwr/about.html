History

Founded in 2010 by seasoned river guide Alex Okoro, Rapids Adventures began with a single raft on the Kaduna River. Driven by passion for Nigeria's waterways and community development, the company grew to become a leading white water rafting destination in West Africa. Today, with expert guides and eco-friendly practices, Rapids Adventures offers thrilling experiences while supporting local conservation efforts.
